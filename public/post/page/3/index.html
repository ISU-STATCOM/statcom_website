About STATCOM @ ISU

Fri Oct 28, 2005 by Amin Shirazi

STATCOM (Statistics in the Community) at Iowa State University is a student-run volunteer organization at the Department of Statistics that provides pro-bono statistical consulting to local organizations that work for the common good. The program offers the expertise of statistics graduate students, free of charge, to non-profit governmental and community organizations in the areas of data organization, analysis, and interpretation.
